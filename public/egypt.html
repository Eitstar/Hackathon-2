A Glimpse Into Egypt History

Ancient Egypt was one of the greatest and most powerful civilizations in the history of the world

It lasted for over 3000 years from 3150 BC to 30 BC

The civilization of Ancient Egypt was located along the Nile River in northeast Africa

The Nile provided food, soil, water, and transportation for the Egyptians.
