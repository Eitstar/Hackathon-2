A business introduction letter is a way for companies to introduce themselves to potential customers

Additionally, company introduction letters may be categorized as either business-to-business, where a company writes to another business, and business-to-customer, where the business writes to its market or clientele

A business-to-business letter of introduction might commonly be used by businesses introducing their products, services or intent to another business

The intent behind this type of business introduction letter may be to describe a possible partnership,

request investment opportunities, or, if a company's niche market is other businesses, they might use an introduction letter to describe the products and services that they offer.
